This is a practice project for exploring Elasticsearch using sample data from the Experimental Factor Ontology - a hierarchical set of terms describing human diseases and their relations.

  • Records can be located by official EFO ID or by disease name, e.g. EFO_0000305 or 'arthritis'.
  • Exact matches in disease name are prioritised followed by partial matches, then matches in disease synonyms.
  • Results are weighted by known drug target associations from Open Targets data.
